The Art of Sound Design

The Audio Engineer!!

July 28, 2019

Master Audio Crafters , Blacksmith of Sounds an experienced A.E. can manipulate sounds however he or she wants. There are key ingredients in a mix, but there are no rules. With the amount of technology and equipment available these days, the combinations are endless. 'The mix should fit like a glove...', it's what my instructor used to say. You should mix with freedom, you should mix with expression but you should also be surgical.
